Who am I?

My name's Lawrence, a senior majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics at Berea College (a highly ranked liberal arts college where no student pays tuition). Not only was I awarded a full-tuition scholarship, but I also gained the opportunity to immerse myself deeply in Mathematics and Computer Science while working 10 hours a week as a Full-Stack Software Engineer on top of personal projects.

During my time as an undergraduate, I've created numerous projects like my Keyboard Overhaul which increases my productivity, and my Build-a Lawrence app which helps me dress my best. I've taken classes on data structures, algorithmic analysis, discrete math, combinatorics, linear algebra, and operating systems, and I haven't shied away from leadership roles either. I was the student manager for hardware repair on campus in the IT department and I now serve as the Vice-President of Code Together where I organize events and tutor students on how to best sharpen their techincal skills. Now I'm looking for full-time roles in Software Engineering and I'm open to relocating.
